历史空间

Historical Space Every time you remember the past, the image in your head is that the natural one or it’s produced by the media? Media is a kind of memory that even redefined our natural function of memory? That’s what I want to say in this work. So I build a virtual pass time space memory by social media and some new data, that will show by the right time in current time, even though it’s used to.
